Who We Are

  • We are Ethiopian and Eritireans Chritians in Pittsbugh.
  • We gather every two weeks on Sunday at the first baptist church for prayer, worship and sermon.
  • We are non-demominational christian felloship and we welcome anyone regardless of race, color, and religion diffences.
  • Our sunday services are mostly in Amharic or Tigrigna, but mix with english since there are people who can't speak Amharic or Tigrigna language
  • We have English bible study every other week on saturdays
  • We have Amharic bible study every other week on sundays
