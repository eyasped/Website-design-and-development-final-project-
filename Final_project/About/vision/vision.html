Our Vision

“Write the vision and make it plain…” Habakkuk 2:2

SPECIFIC GOALS TO ACCOMPLISH THE VISION

These goals are vision, ministry plan and core values of our fellowship.

  1. Grow The Fellowship

    We believe that God wants us to reach out to an ever increasing percentage of the unchurched region of the pittsburgh with the gospel message of Jesus Christ.

      Our Plan

      Focus on biblical preaching and teaching of God’s Word. Emphasis on relational evangelism training for everyone.
    1. Increased emphasis on preaching/teaching on relational evangelism.
    2. Relational evangelism courses.
    3. Testimonies of relational successes.
      Provide outreaches annually on an pittsburgh region
      Building relationships with unchurched people outside the family of God that goes across racial and social-economic boundaries.

  2. Develope the fellowship

    1. community
        Move our fellowship of Christ followers toward community, spiritual maturity, and full participation within the life of the fellowship.
        Develop and train children and youth leaders and workers in providing effective and relevant ministry to young families.
        Develop a leadership development program that gives a clear leadership path in becoming a leader.

      Our Plan

        Develop and train small group leaders to lead:
      1. Connect Groups (Fellowship and Recreational
      2. Reach Groups (Reaching Out)
      3. Serve Groups (Using Our Gifts to Serve)
    2. Spiritual Maturity
        Develop and train a worship ministry that leads the fellowship in authentic worship,
        Development of GROW Groups “Classes and Training” that will provide Leadership Path Classes:
      1. Spiritual Foundations
      2. Spiritual Gifts Class
      3. Small Group Leadership
      4. Relational Evangelism
        Develop and train a worship ministry that leads the fellowship in authentic worship,
      1. Lead the congregation in prayer by:
        1. having prayer and fasting times as a fellowship
        2. Prayer focus in ALL small groups (ministries of the church).
      2. Lead the fellowship in Bible reading by:
        1. Having open Bibles and good note taking during services and classes.
        2. Lead and encourage focused Bible reading and personal devotional times.
